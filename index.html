FAQ

Does ShieldShot send my screenshots anywhere?

No. Detection, blurring, editing and export all happen inside your browser. The extension makes exactly one kind of network request: validating your license key with Gumroad when you activate Pro.

What does it detect automatically?

Email addresses, phone numbers, IBANs, credit card numbers and Spanish DNI/NIE identifiers. You can pixelate anything else manually in the editor.

Is it really a one-time payment?

Yes. €19 once, use it forever, including updates. No account required — just a license key.

Does it work on Edge / Brave / Arc?

Yes, any Chromium-based browser that supports Chrome extensions.

Can I get a refund?

If ShieldShot doesn't work for you, reply to your purchase email within 14 days and you'll get a full refund.
